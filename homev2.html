Between the Lines

Between the Lines is a creative intervention responding to the lived experiences of international students who have been impacted by the influence of the UK border control system on Higher Education Institutions.

Between the Lines uses DNA data storage technology to embed stories from the international student community back into the Higher Education administrative systems that govern them.
