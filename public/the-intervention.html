The Intervention

Between the Lines infiltrates the administrative systems of Higher Education institutions throughout the UK.

The pens, along with the lived experiences they hold embedded within the ink, have been posted out to the administration hubs of all HE Student Sponsors.

Along with the pens, the recipient receives a letter inviting them to be the new keeper of the pen - should they choose to accept the responsibility it carries.

The new pen keepers are instructed to use the pens when undertaking administrative tasks on behalf of their institution, so that the lived experiences of those impacted can permeate the administrative systems that govern them.

As the keepers use the pens, the stories within the ink transfer to the paper - embedding the lived experiences of the international students back into the administrative systems that govern them.
