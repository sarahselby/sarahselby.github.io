The System

'Between the Lines' interrogates the UK border regime. The border regime is a name for the overall system that tries to control people’s ability to move and live, depending on our immigration status (Corporate Watch, 2018). Though we often consider 'borders' to mean geographical boundaries, the UK border control regime is a distributed system that relies on the collaboration (often inadvertently) of many.
